QUADRA Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

QUADRA is a local, single-device board game designed not to collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

What we collect

Nothing.

What we share

Nothing.

Third-party services

QUADRA does not use any third-party services. It contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporting, and no cloud sync. It makes no network requests during normal gameplay.

Data storage on your device

QUADRA may store player names and settings locally on your device. This data never leaves the device and is removed if you uninstall the app.

Children's privacy

QUADRA is suitable for all ages and collects no data from any user regardless of age.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Leave a review on the Google Play Store and we'll get back to you.
